Who we are

Most teams treat testing as the thing you rush at the end. We treat quality as a discipline that runs through the whole build — and that difference is what keeps products stable, fast and trustworthy once real users arrive.

ITIE partners with founders and product teams who can't afford for their software to break in front of customers. Whether you're shipping your first MVP or scaling a platform across thousands of users, we bring senior QA judgement to the table: what to test, how deeply, with which tools, and where the real risk lives.

We work as an extension of your team — embedding into your sprints, plugging into your CI, and reporting in plain language your developers and stakeholders both understand. From manual exploratory testing to fully automated regression suites, from API load testing to Web3 and AI coverage, we meet your product where it is.

Approach

How we work with you

A simple, transparent process — whether it's a one-off audit or an embedded partnership.

Step 01

Understand

Discovery & scoping
  • We learn your product, users, stack and where quality matters most.
  • We review existing requirements, test cases and any current QA process.
  • We identify the highest-risk areas to prioritise first.
Step 02

Strategise

Test plan & strategy
  • We draft a clear test strategy: what we'll test, how, with which tools, and what "done" looks like.
  • We agree priorities, timelines and reporting cadence with you.
  • We decide where automation pays off and where manual testing is smarter.
Step 03

Execute

Testing & automation
  • We run functional, API, performance, cross-platform and accessibility testing as scoped.
  • We build automation suites that integrate with your CI.
  • We log defects with clear, reproducible steps developers can act on immediately.
Step 04

Report & improve

Visibility & handover
  • You get clear test summary reports and a prioritised defect list.
  • We work with your developers through to resolution.
  • We can train your team to sustain quality after the engagement ends.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes — much of our experience is exactly that. Through GIZ we've advised 20+ early and growth-stage startups, so we're comfortable bringing quality discipline to products that are still finding their feet, on realistic budgets.

Can you work with our existing tools and CI?

Absolutely. We integrate with your stack rather than forcing ours on you — automation suites plug into your CI pipeline (e.g. Jenkins), and defects flow into the tracker your team already uses, whether that's Jira, Asana or ClickUp.

Do you do one-off testing or only ongoing work?

Both. You can engage us for a single project — an audit, a release, an automation build — or as an embedded QA partner across your sprints. See the engagement models above.

What about Web3 and AI products?

These are core strengths, not afterthoughts. We test wallets, smart contracts and nodes for decentralized apps, and conversation flows, accuracy and APIs for AI chatbots and assistants.

Will our developers actually understand the reports?

Yes. We log defects with clear, reproducible steps and deliver test summary reports in plain language — so both engineers and stakeholders know exactly what's wrong, how serious it is, and what to do next.
